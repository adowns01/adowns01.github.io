Revisiting "Affirming your Values"

In my post about affirming my values, I wrote about the following four values:

  • Adventure
  • Autonomy
  • Confidence
  • Independence

In my blog post about stereotype threat I wrote about my experiences in male dominated fields. I think the values I listed above are important to me (especially confidence) because those are the values that I try to use when I feel the effects of stereotype threat.

When I am feeling down at DBC, I hope that I will feel safe around my peers so that I can turn to them for comfort or help.
