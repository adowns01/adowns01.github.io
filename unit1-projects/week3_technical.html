Why is JavaScript so popular in web development?

JavaScript allows websites to be interactive! Things can move, information can update, and pop-ups can pop-up! Or in technical terms, it can make a website dynamic.

  • Do you want the current date and time on your webpage? Use JavaScript.
  • Do you want a pop-up to annoy your viewers every 10 seconds? Use JavaScript.
  • Do you want a secure on-screen keyboard? Use JavaScript.

Besides the fact that it can make your webpage awesome (or really annoying) here are some other great things about JS:

  • It works on every major browser.
  • You don't compile it.
  • It's high level (looks more like english and less like binary), so it's easy to learn, even for people who have never coded before.
